Brand architecture

Keep the structure simple while the estate is still sharpening.

Elusion is the root idea. Elusion Works is the operating layer. Product names keep their own identity only where audience, purpose, or market genuinely demands it.

Tier 3

Named properties

Sites like AI Resource Hub or Axy Lusion keep their own names only because they behave like destinations.

Keep

Distinctive property names where they make the estate easier to understand, remember, and navigate.

Avoid

Inventing layers of brands for capabilities, services, or internal tools that do not need to look like companies.

Use later

Create new sub-brands only when the audience, product shape, and revenue model justify the complexity.

Default move

If something is a capability or operating system, keep it under Elusion Works and let the main brand get stronger.

About

A digital studio should feel intelligent, refined, and slightly charged.

Elusion Works should read like a serious creative-technology business without feeling stale, corporate, or over-designed. The site should feel forged, not decorated.

That means stronger hierarchy, sharper typography, visible mechanics, and motion that creates presence instead of gimmicks.

Current posture

  • The front end is now designed around a darker, forged brand direction.
  • The structure is intentionally simple enough to hold deeper case studies later.
  • The estate index is live now; richer product narratives can grow underneath it.
  • Once the final production logo lands, the site can be tightened again around it.
