About

Why Elusion Works exists.

Elusion Works is the umbrella brand for digital products, publishing systems, creative technology, and future interactive work built by Kol Tregaskes.

Elusion is the root idea: digital creation, transformation, invention, and interactive craft. Works keeps the name broad enough to cover websites, tools, media, and games without boxing the brand into one narrow category.
