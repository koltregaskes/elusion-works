Inside the
Golden Retriever

Intelligent, affectionate, and eager to please — the Golden Retriever is one of the world's most beloved companions. Bred in Scotland for retrieving game on land and water, Goldens excel as family dogs, service animals, and working partners.

Temperament

Goldens are friendly, gentle, and highly intelligent. They are known for their patience with children and sociable nature with people and other dogs.

Learn More

Exercise

A moderately high-energy breed that needs daily activity to stay happy and healthy. Aim for 60+ minutes of exercise and mental stimulation.

Learn More

Grooming

Golden Retrievers have a dense, water-repellent double coat that sheds year-round. Regular brushing keeps their coat healthy and your home a little cleaner.

Learn More

Nutrition

A balanced diet tailored to their life stage, activity level, and sensitivities supports lifelong health and vitality.

Learn More

Health

Generally a healthy breed, but prone to certain genetic conditions. Responsible breeding, proper care, and regular vet checkups make a big difference.

Learn More

Training

Eager to please and food motivated, Goldens excel at training. Use positive reinforcement and keep sessions fun and consistent.
