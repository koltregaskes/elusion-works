What we collect

The newsletter and contact surfaces are non-sending demo interactions. They do not create a real subscription, support ticket, or message record.

What we don't collect

  • We don't use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We don't fingerprint your browser or device.
  • We don't sell, trade, or rent your data to anyone.

Cookies

This demo does not intentionally set cookies. If that changes, the note should be updated before launch.

Your rights

If this prototype is ever converted into a real publication, a proper privacy policy and contact route must be added before collecting personal data.

Data retention

No production retention period is defined because the demo does not store newsletter subscriptions or contact messages.

Children

The demo is intended for general viewing and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If the demo becomes a live product, this page should be replaced with a production privacy policy before launch.
