What we collect

If you subscribe to the newsletter, we store your email address. If you write to us, we store your message. We use privacy-respecting analytics (Plausible) to count page views; no cookies, no individual tracking.

What we don't collect

  • We don't use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We don't fingerprint your browser or device.
  • We don't sell, trade, or rent your data to anyone.

Cookies

We set one functional cookie to remember your reading preferences. We don't use any analytics, advertising, or social cookies.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can request a copy of any data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete it, or withdraw consent at any time. Email privacy@goldenencyclopedia.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

Data retention

Newsletter subscriptions are kept until you unsubscribe. Contact form messages are retained for 24 months for follow-up, then deleted.

Children

This site is suitable for general audiences but we don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we'll delete it immediately.

Changes

If we change this policy materially we'll note it at the top of this page and notify newsletter subscribers by email.
