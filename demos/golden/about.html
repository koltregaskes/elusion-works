Why we made this

Most breed information online is shallow, recycled, or written for ad placement rather than the dog. We wanted a single, beautiful, trustworthy reference for the Golden Retriever — one a new owner could read in an afternoon and keep returning to for years.

How we work

Every article is reviewed by at least one veterinarian and one experienced trainer before it goes live. We cite primary sources — peer-reviewed studies and breed-club guidance — wherever facts are contested. We update content on a rolling basis as new research emerges.

  • Veterinary review on health, nutrition, and lifespan content
  • Trainer review on behaviour and training content
  • Photographer credits on every original image
  • Public changelog for major content revisions

What we don't do

We don't sell breeders' listings, run sponsored content, or take payment for product placement. The newsletter is free; the site has no paywall. If we ever do partner with a brand, it will be clearly labelled and editorially independent.
