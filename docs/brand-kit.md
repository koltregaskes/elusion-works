# Elusion Works Brand Kit

## Core Positioning

Elusion Works is the umbrella label for websites, tools, game ideas, and future services built
by Kol Tregaskes.

## Brand Logic

- Public-facing references should use `Elusion Works`.
- `Elusion` remains the underlying brand idea: invention, transformation, and interactive craft.
- `Works` keeps the name broad enough for products, software, media, portfolio work, and future services.

## Launch Positioning Lines

Primary:

`Elusion Works is a showcase for websites, tools, and game ideas by Kol Tregaskes.`

Longer option:

`Elusion Works is the front door for a growing portfolio of websites, internal systems, and future interactive work.`
